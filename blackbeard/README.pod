=head1 NAME

Blackbeard :: A DVD & Blue-Ray Conversion Suite for MPlayer

=head1 SYNOPSIS

        Tell your environment where BB is located:

        export BB_HOME=/path/to/blackbeard

	Rip your movie from disk:

	bbrip -o file.vob

	Learn important details about your rip:

	bbinfo -e -i file.vob

        Convert your rip into a compressed movie:

        bbencode -i file.vob -o file.avi  


=head1 DESCRIPTION

Blackbeard (BB) is a DVD & Blue-Ray conversion suite for MPlayer. BB currently provides support for FFmpeg, x264 and Snow video compression. With additional support for mp2, mp3, ac3 and faac audio compression. BB provides a wide range of conversion options that take advantage of complex automation algorithm's.
